About Us

Mahunyal Secondary School is committed to providing quality education to students from grades 1 to 12. Located in Bardagoriya RM-06, Baskoti Kailali, our school aims to foster an environment that promotes academic excellence, character development, and personal growth.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students to achieve their full potential by providing a supportive and challenging learning environment. We strive to cultivate critical thinking skills, creativity, and a lifelong love for learning.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading educational institution recognized for its academic excellence, innovation, and commitment to holistic development. We aspire to nurture responsible and compassionate individuals who contribute positively to society.
